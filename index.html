Business plan

Market

Bitcoin and crypto is still early. However change is happening. Banks, governments, retail and individuals are all looking into the possibilities of these new technologies. SytacDefi offers support in these commercial and non-commercial area's. We offer general and one-to-one guidance enabling anyone to start with blockchain, bitcoin or crypto. This support is done with partners, third-party solutions or external advisors (legal) to provide our customers the best options and insights.

Marketing

Sytac positions itself as the thought-leader of blockchain, bitcoin and crypto. We present, lecture and tell practical stories in the public. As key-note speakers, we create awareness of the services Sytac provides. Our target group is anyone with interest in Blockchain; corporates, small businesses or even individuals. It is important to broadcast the Sytac brand first in the Netherlands, then Germany and in the end Europe.

Finance

Until Q1 2022 we start simple, creating the fundament and sorting things out. Creating awareness within the Sytac crew, and get support from intrinsic motivated developers. Creating awareness outside the Sytac domain by organising meetup's and creating content on our off-label bliksem.live blog website. Attracting commercial assignments and investors to accelerate SytacDefi to the moon (and back).

Operations

Hosting and Developers are provided by Sytac to built proof-of-concepts showcases, presentations or other material products. The startup is located in A'dam and operates under the Sytac brand. Duncan Bloem is initiator and lead developer of this initiative, but also provides services to Sytac in general if necessary. The cooperation between the existing business and the new startup should bring synergy for both.

No out-of-pocket costs should be made, except for promotional equipment.
